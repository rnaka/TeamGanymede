The Ambient produce is currently a JavaFX app that shows a gray circle upon first usage. When a value between 0 and 99 is entered into the textbox, the color of the circle will change depending on the value entered.

Colors Guide
Gray: The starting color
Red: Any positive value less than and equal to 25
Purple: Any Value between 25 to 50, excluding 25
Green: Any value between 50 and 75, excluding 50
Yellow: Any Value betweeen 75 and 99, excluding 75
